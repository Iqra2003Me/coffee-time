Our Branches








Kaffiene

Inspired by the amazing coffee culture in Australia and New Zealand, Kaffeine is an independently owned cafe and espresso bar with two locations in London's Fitzrovia, just 5 minutes walk from Oxford Circus or Tottenham Court Road. Discover them for yourself. Breakfast, lunch or special baked treats from weekly changing menus with free delivery in Fitzrovia and SOHO.Get your favourite Kaffeine products without having to get up from your couch.







Espresso

Espresso is a family owned chain of coffee shops first opening its doors in Zamzama in 2006 with a tiny 18 seater Cafe. In a time when 'going for coffee' was unheard of, Espresso paved the way of this new tradition, being the first and only true coffee house of its time serving authenic Italian espresso - based drinks and a cafe style menu.We serve our breakfast all day, so you can choose what time you call morning. Feel like an omelette in the afternoon? Give us a call or order online, we'll have it sent over to you in a jiffy!







New York Coffee

New York Coffee is known for its delicious coffee. With its own gourmet blends made from highest quality coffee beans roasted to perfection at the brand's own roastery in Sitra, Bahrain, the brand never fails to draw in coffee connoisseurs and enthusiasts. Take your coffee black or as part of a coffee drinks such as a latte or a caramel macchiato. Don't forget to sample some of the brand's signature drinks: New York Subway Special combines a shot of espresso, caramel and hazelnut syrup, steamed milk and whipped cream.







Small ST.Cafe

Serving amazing espresso, filter, cakes & sarnies in Bristol's Old City since 2012. House roast by Clifton Coffee, guest roasters from Bristol, the UK, and beyond. Proud to serve Bristol-made breads, cakes & pastries.reat little place, warm and inviting, staff friendly. Absolutely love the coffee books on the shelf you can take a look through while sipping good quality coffee! An intimate cafe in which the coffee machine and the various blends on offer take centre stage. Relaxed atmosphere and a great place to start the day.







Hempstory

The one and only International Coffee Shop around Amsterdam. A clean and green oasis in the often vibrant and busy city center of Amsterdam. This is where we show and sell a wide range of Drinking products all connected to the healthy, sustainable side of the Coffee. Feel welcome in our store and take in the calming and brightening atmosphere. Take a seat and enjoy for a delicious hemp drink or have a chat with us. We're a small team of women, all passionate about the plant and loving to explain you all about it. We are here to share our love for the plant and the planet
